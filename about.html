About QRCraft

Free QR Code Generator

QRCraft is a free, open-source QR code generator built for speed and privacy. Create customizable QR codes for URLs, text, email addresses, phone numbers, Wi-Fi credentials, and more — all without creating an account or uploading your data to a server.

Why QRCraft?

  • 100% Client-Side — Everything runs in your browser. No data leaves your device.
  • Customizable — Custom colors, logo embedding, adjustable size, and error correction levels.
  • Batch Generation — Generate up to 50 QR codes at once and download them as a ZIP.
  • Multiple Formats — Export as PNG for web or SVG for print.
  • No Account Required — No sign-up, no watermarks, no limits.
  • Free & Open Source — Source code available on GitHub.
