Free Online QR Code Generator

QRCraft is a free QR code generator that lets you create customizable QR codes instantly. Generate QR codes for URLs, text, email addresses, phone numbers, Wi-Fi credentials, and more. All processing happens in your browser — no data is uploaded to any server.

How to Use

  • Enter Content — Type or paste any text, URL, or data into the input field.
  • Customize Colors — Choose custom foreground and background colors using the color pickers or hex input.
  • Add a Logo — Upload an image to embed in the center of your QR code. Error correction is automatically set to High to ensure scannability.
  • Adjust Settings — Set the error correction level (Low, Medium, Quartile, High) and output size (100–2000px).
  • Generate — Click the button or press Ctrl+Enter to create your QR code.

Batch Mode

Switch to Batch mode to generate up to 50 QR codes at once. Paste one entry per line — each line becomes its own QR code. Download all codes as a ZIP file or save them individually.

Download & Export

Download your QR code as a PNG image or SVG vector file. You can also copy the QR code image directly to your clipboard. SVG exports are resolution-independent and perfect for print materials.

Error Correction Levels

  • Low (7%) — Smallest QR code, minimal damage tolerance.
  • Medium (15%) — Balanced size and error recovery. Recommended for most uses.
  • Quartile (25%) — Good damage tolerance for printed codes.
  • High (30%) — Maximum recovery, required when embedding a logo.

Works on desktop and mobile browsers. No account required, no watermarks, completely free.
